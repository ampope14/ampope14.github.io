PAW'S PLACE

-Where Your Pets are in Pawesome Care!

At Paw's Place, we have a passion for caring for dogs. Happy, healthy pups are at the heart of who we are. We’ve created a loving place with what dogs want and need in mind. It’s a dog daycare, boarding and spa facility all rolled into one!

Top benefits of doggie day care centers x264 from Denisemary on Vimeo.

Paw's Place is dedicated to offering exceptional care for dogs and currently operates in Exeter, New Hampshire. Our modern facilities provide a healthy, safe and fun environment for dogs to socialize and play in with over 3,000 square feet in play areas. Our multiple, spacious playrooms allow us to separate our dogs and cats by size and temperaments. The compressed rubber flooring aids in the long-term joint health of your furry friend, as well as offering a higher level of sanitization. Get peace of mind knowing you are leaving your furry family member in the best care possible.
